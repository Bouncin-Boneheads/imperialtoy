  History
 
Imperial Toy Corporation was founded in 1969.
  Description
 
Imperial Toy Corporation has showrooms in Los Angeles, New York, Toronto and Hong Kong with additional production and distribution facilities in other cities.

Wide-spread distribution outside of North America spans the globe throughout Europe, Scandinavia, Latin America, Australia and South Africa.

  Outlets
 
Imperial Toys are sold in all of the leading national chain outlets.
  Products
 
A broad range of popular product lines includes 500 year-round and seasonal items. Major products include billions of marbles and tens of millions of Miracle Bubbles™ sold, flying discs, water squirters, 40 different bubble toys, and jump ropes.
  Other Featured Brands
 
Imperial Toy currently has one of the most poplular categories for girls; Petite Miss®. Petite Miss cosmetics have been in the marketplace for fourteen years. It is featured at all national chain outlets as well as at grocery stores, department stores, drug stores and specialty stores. Product line includes lipgloss, compacts, perfume and other make-up. This category has gained popularity worldwide.
  Product Pricing
 
Low-price category leaders in the 15¢ to $4 range. Also offer a full complement of mid-priced toys at $5, $10 and $15.
  Present Licenses
 
Popular licenses include Ford, Chevrolet, various roush racing teams, Quaker State, and Tosco/Unocal.
